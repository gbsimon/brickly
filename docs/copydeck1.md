# Brickly -- Landing Page Copy Deck (Playful Draft)

## Positioning
Brickly helps you rebuild LEGO sets faster by turning any parts inventory into a clean, tap-happy checklist you can use right at the build table.

## Primary User Segments
- Adult fans of LEGO (AFOLs) rebuilding vintage or partial sets
- Parents rebuilding sets with kids from mixed bins
- Collectors restoring sets before resale or display
- MOC builders who break down and rebuild sets repeatedly

## Top Pain Points
- Inventory lists are hard to manage on paper or in spreadsheets
- Missing pieces slow the build and break momentum
- Large sets are overwhelming to track part-by-part
- Progress gets lost across devices or sessions
- Parts lists are hard to filter when you're mid-build

## Core Benefits (Outcomes)
- Find pieces faster with a clean, touch-friendly checklist
- Stay organized on big sets with filters, sorting, and color groups
- Keep momentum with quick count controls and hide-completed
- Build anywhere with offline mode and auto-sync later
- Pick up right where you left off on any device

## Messaging Pillars
- Clarity: See exactly what's left
- Speed: Tap to track parts in seconds
- Reliability: Offline-first, progress never lost
- Focus: Designed for the build table

## Features
- Search sets and add them to your library
- Part lists with color-grouped sections
- Increment/decrement counters for each part
- Hide completed parts with a single toggle
- Offline support with local caching
- Multi-device sync for library and progress
- English and French support
- Accessibility-friendly interface (large targets, keyboard support)

---

## How It Works
1) Find your set -- Search by name or set number and add it to your library.
2) Track parts as you build -- Tap to increment found pieces and hide completed parts.
3) Pick up anywhere -- Your progress is saved offline and syncs across devices.

## FAQ (Draft)

**Do I need an internet connection?**
No. You can build offline and sync when you're back online.

**Can I use it on my phone or desktop?**
Yes--Brickly works on any modern browser, with an iPad-first layout.

**Does it include minifigs?**
Yes, minifigs appear in the inventory list.

**Can I hide parts once I find them?**
Yes, there's a toggle to hide completed parts.
